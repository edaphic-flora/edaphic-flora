# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: January 2025**

Edaphic Flora ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our soil data application.

## Information We Collect

### Account Information
- **Email address** - Used for authentication and to associate your data submissions with your account
- **Authentication provider data** - If you sign in with Google, we receive your name and email from Google

### Soil Data You Submit
- Species and cultivar names
- Soil test values (pH, nutrients, texture, etc.)
- Location coordinates (latitude/longitude)
- Sample dates and notes
- Outcome observations (thriving, struggling, etc.)

### Automatically Collected
- Basic usage analytics (pages visited, features used)
- Browser type and device information for compatibility

## How We Use Your Information

- **To provide the service** - Store and display your soil data, generate visualizations
- **To improve the database** - Aggregate anonymized data for community analysis
- **To communicate** - Send important updates about the service (rarely)
- **To process uploads** - Soil report files are sent to AI services (Anthropic Claude) for data extraction, then deleted

## Data Sharing and Visibility

### Your Submitted Soil Data
- **Is publicly visible** in aggregate analyses and maps
- **Is shared** under the [CC BY-NC 4.0 license](https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by-nc/4.0/) for non-commercial use
- **Your email is NOT publicly displayed** - only used internally for attribution

### We Do NOT
- Sell your personal information
- Share your email with third parties for marketing
- Use your data for purposes unrelated to Edaphic Flora

## Third-Party Services

We use the following services that may process your data:

| Service | Purpose | Their Privacy Policy |
|---------|---------|---------------------|
| Firebase (Google) | Authentication | [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy) |
| Neon | Database hosting | [Neon Privacy Policy](https://neon.tech/privacy) |
| Anthropic Claude | AI extraction of soil reports | [Anthropic Privacy Policy](https://www.anthropic.com/privacy) |

## Data Retention

- **Account data** - Retained while your account is active
- **Soil submissions** - Retained indefinitely as part of the community database
- **Uploaded files** - Processed for extraction and immediately deleted (not stored)

## Your Rights

You may:
- **Access** your submitted data through the app
- **Edit or delete** your own submissions at any time
- **Request account deletion** by contacting us

## Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures including:
- Encrypted connections (HTTPS)
- Secure database hosting with access controls
- No storage of passwords (handled by Firebase)

## Children's Privacy

Edaphic Flora is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. Significant changes will be noted in the app or via email.
