Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

What We Collect

When you create an account, we store your email address for authentication and attribution. When you submit soil samples, we store the data you provide (species, soil values, location coordinates, and notes).

How We Use Your Data

Soil sample data is shared with other users to build a community reference database. Your email is used for account management and is not publicly displayed. We do not sell or share personal information with third parties.

Data License

All submitted soil data is shared under a CC BY-NC 4.0 license, freely available for non-commercial use by researchers, gardeners, and educators.

Cookies & Analytics

We use essential cookies for authentication (via Firebase/Polished). We do not use third-party tracking or advertising cookies.
